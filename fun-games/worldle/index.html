Worldle — The Daily Word Puzzle

Worldle is a free daily word game where you have six tries to guess a hidden five-letter word. A brand-new puzzle unlocks every day at midnight, and everyone plays the same word — so you can compare results with friends.

After each guess, the colour of the tiles tells you how close you are. Switch to Practice mode any time for unlimited random puzzles.

Objective & Gameplay

Guess the WORD in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. After you submit, the tiles change colour to show how good your guess was:

  • 🟩 Green — the letter is correct and in the right spot.
  • 🟨 Yellow — the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • ⬜ Gray — the letter is not in the word at all.
